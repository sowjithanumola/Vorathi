What is SowLang?

More than words.
A new voice.

SowLang (Sowāvēna in its own tongue) is a constructed spoken language — a conlang — designed from the ground up to be expressive, phonetically rich, and emotionally resonant.

Inspired by the musicality of Telugu, the tonal clarity of Mandarin, the flowing vowels of Italian, and the logical structure of Esperanto, SowLang weaves these influences into something entirely original.

It features 28 consonants, 12 vowels, 5 distinct tones, and a Subject-Verb-Object word order — making it accessible to speakers of most world languages while offering depth for advanced expression.
